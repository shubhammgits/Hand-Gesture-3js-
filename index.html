Instructions
This is a hand gesture controlled particle planet built with Three.js + MediaPipe Hands.
Allow camera access when prompted. If you blocked it earlier, enable Camera permission in your browser settings and reload.
Gestures: pinch (thumb + index) to zoom in/out. Rotate your hand to rotate the planet. Use good lighting and keep one hand in frame.
Audio: some browsers block autoplay. If sound is muted, tap once or press PLAY AUDIO.
Live website: https://shubhammgits.github.io/Hand-Gesture-3js-/
